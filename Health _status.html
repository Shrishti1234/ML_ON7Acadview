EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS BY SHRISHTI¶

Our project is to analyse which factors affect most to health status of people.Health of peaople is analysed on basis of different characteristics like age factor,financial status of person, race and hispanic origin of person , gender etc.

Poverty is the major reason for poor health status of people in country like India.As data set does not include children less than 15 years because they does not have income source so this data shows health status of people above 18 or under 18 age.

The data set has 12 variables.One variable is of characteristics on which basis health status is claculated and other varibles are showing percentage of health status and standard error .

DATA DESCRIPTION¶

Dataset is of health status on basis of selected characteristics which effect or responsible for health status of person.Dataset have 12 data variables.One input variable is of characteristics oh which basis heath status of person is clasified as excellent, good ,poor etc. Other 11 variables shows status heath status estimate and error.Standard error estimates were calculated using replicate weights,'Fay's method'.

ATTRIBUTE INFORMATION¶

Following are the input variables in dataset:

  1. Characteristics
  2. Total_number
  3. Excellent_estimate
  4. Excellent_standard
  5. very_good_estimate
  6. very_good_standard
  7. good_estimate
  8. good_standard
  9. Fair_estimate
  10. Fair_standard_error
  11. Poor_estimate
  12. Poor_standard_error
